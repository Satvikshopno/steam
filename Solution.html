ABOUT SEPL

SEPL is a leading manufacturer of Steam & Water Analysis Systems (SWAS), Continuous Emissions Monitoring Systems (CEMS), Ambient Air Monitoring Systems (AQMS), Water & Waste Water Monitoring, Flow Metering, Vibration Monitoring Systems (VMS), Moisture & Dew Point monitoring systems. We provide complete systems including software / SCADA / PLC requirements for your requirements which even include Shelter / Container based systems for Safe or Hazardous area.

With manufacturing and/or testing facilities in Pune, Bangalore, South Africa, UAE, USA and Singapore, we can cater to your requirements of integrated process monitoring systems as per your needs. With exports to more than 40 countries, we are poised for a bigger growth with the help of our esteemed customers.

Our team of young professionals having varied domain expertise such as Environment Monitoring, Process Gas Analysis, Power Plant Instrumentation, Flow Metering, Dew Point and Moisture Analysis, SWAS, CEMS, AQMS, Vibration Monitoring and Waste to Power generation will come handy for you in improving your processes, increasing throughput and reducing waste; all this with an environment concern.
